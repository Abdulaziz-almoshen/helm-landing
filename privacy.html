Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026

Helm is a product of Ruwad ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Helm mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Helm, we collect:

  • Account information: Name and email address (via Google or Apple sign-in)
  • Content you create: Text, voice recordings, images, and files you capture in the app
  • AI-processed data: Tasks, summaries, and insights generated from your content
  • Usage data: App interactions and feature usage for improving the service
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Helm service
  • To process your content using AI for knowledge extraction, task management, and organization
  • To improve our service and develop new features
  • To send you service-related notifications

3. AI Data Processing

Helm uses AI services to process your content. Your inputs (text, voice transcriptions, and images) are sent securely to AI processing services to extract tasks, generate summaries, and organize your knowledge. This processing is performed solely to deliver the Helm service to you.

If you enable the "AI Data Retention" setting, AI service providers may use your data to improve their models. You can disable this at any time in Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Authentication: Clerk (for Google and Apple sign-in)
  • AI Processing: Third-party AI model providers (for content analysis and knowledge extraction)
  • Error Tracking: Sentry (for crash reporting and diagnostics)
  • Infrastructure: Google Cloud Platform (for hosting and data storage)

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure. We use encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all data transfers. Access to your data is restricted to authenticated users within your workspace.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data (topics, threads, tasks, AI-generated content, and workspace data) is permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Opt out of AI data retention (Settings > AI Data Retention toggle)
  • Control notification preferences

8. Children's Privacy

Helm is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
