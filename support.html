Troubleshooting

Content is not processing
Ensure you have an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app, then resubmit your capture.
Voice recording is not working
Make sure you have granted Helm microphone permission. Go to your device Settings > Helm > Microphone and ensure it is enabled.
App is running slowly
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, ensure your device has the latest iOS update installed and contact us at abdulaziz@ruwad.ai.
