Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI processing work?

When you capture content (voice, text, or images), Helm's AI analyzes it to extract tasks, decisions, and summaries. Processing requires an internet connection.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS and stored securely on cloud infrastructure. Your data is workspace-scoped and only accessible to authenticated members.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings (tap your avatar) > scroll to "Delete Account". This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Does Helm work offline?

You can capture content offline. It will sync and process when you reconnect to the internet.

What languages are supported?

Helm supports English and Arabic. The AI automatically detects your input language and responds accordingly.
